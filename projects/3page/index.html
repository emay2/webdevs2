How To Download An App

  1. Open the App Store app.
  2. Tap the Search icon at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Enter the app name that you are looking for.
  4. Tap Search on your keyboard.
  5. Tap the app or game you wish to purchase or download for free.
  6. Tap Get if it is free, or the Price of the app if it is paid.
  7. Tap Install or Buy when the icon switches.
